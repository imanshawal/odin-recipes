Ingredients:

  • 6 sprigs fresh mint, divided
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 3 ounces white rum
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can lime-flavored seltzer water

Directions:

  1. Muddle 4 mint sprigs and sugar together in a mixing glass until crushed.
  2. Add rum and stir to dissolve sugar.
  3. Strain into 2 ice-filled Collins or rocks glasses.
  4. Top with seltzer and garnish each glass with a remaining mint sprig.
